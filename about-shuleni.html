About Shuleni

Every school dreams of a simple way to manage report cards - one that saves time, reduces paperwork, and keeps everything accurate. That dream inspired the birth of Shuleni.

At first, it started as a samll idea: What if teachers could record marks, calculate grades, and generateclean, printable report cards - all in one place? We built Shuleni to answer that question.

Today, Shuleni has grown into a complete Report Card Management System designed for that want to embrace digital record keeping without the stress of complicated software. It's easy to use, works offline, and puts the entire reporting process - from entering marks to grading students - into one smooth flow.

Our mission is simple: to make school reporting faster, smarter, and more reliable.
No more missing records. No more manual calculations. Just clean data, ready to share and print.

At Shuleni, we believe technology should support teachers, not replace them. That's why every feature we design aims to make your work lighter, your results clearer, and your students' progress easier to follow.

Welcome to Shuleni - where report cards meet simplicity.
