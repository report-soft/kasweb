Features of Shuleni

Shuleni is built for Ugandan schools to simplify report card preparation. Key features include:

  • ✔ Generate professional report cards with school logo and term details.
  • ✔ Print or export to PDF with a single click.
  • ✔ Import student marks from Excel or CSV files.
  • ✔ Export student marks to Excel or CSV files.
  • ✔ Automatic grade calculation based on Ugandan grading systems.
  • ✔ Works offline on your school’s computers — no internet required.
