How to Register, Promote & Transfer Learners with EMIS in Uganda

The Education Management Information System (EMIS) is a digital system established by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MoES) to collect, process, store, and analyze education-related data from all institutions of learning in Uganda.

What Schools can do on the EMIS Portal

With the EMIS system, schools can register, promote, and transfer their learners. Schools can also add and transfer staff (Teaching & Non-teaching), and manage infrastructure.

How to Add Learners to EMIS (Baseline Learner Registration)

  1. Go to the menu on your Dashboard and select the EMIS DATA UPLOAD tab.

  2. Click Upload Data, then click the Update button.

  3. Select Learners from the form and click the Update Learner’s Information button.

  4. To add a single learner using UNEB details: Select the Class and Exam Year (PLE/UCE).

  5. Enter the learner’s PLE/UCE Index Number and click Verify.

  6. Confirm the details and fill the remaining fields, including information on orphanage status, district of birth, health issues, special needs, talents, and parent/guardian NIN details.

    • If the learner is a Foreign non-refugee, choose Nationality and enter the Student Pass.

    • If the learner is a Refugee, choose Yes and enter the Refugee ID Number.

Note: The system will auto-generate a Learner Identification Number (LIN) upon confirmation.

How to Promote Learners to the next Class in EMIS

  1. Select Learners from the EMIS dashboard menu.

  2. Click Promotions (this displays a page with Pending and Completed promotions).

  3. Ensure the current Academic Year is active/selected.

  4. Select the Class first, and click the Apply button to load learners’ information.

  5. Choose the Learner(s) using the check box and click the Promote button.

  6. Select the Promotion Status (Promote or Repeat) and the Reporting Status (Reported or Not Reported) from the drop-downs.

  7. Complete the process by clicking the Update button.

How to Transfer Learners in EMIS

The user can transfer learners from the current school to another through the following steps.

  1. Go to the EMIS Dashboard on the left.

  2. Select the Learners module and select Transfers to transfer learners.

  3. View either Incoming transfers or Outgoing transfers.

  4. Click the Transfer Learner button and enter the LIN or NIN of the learner.

  5. Click the Proceed button.

  6. Enter the following learner details to transfer the learner: Class, Transfer Reason, Parent Relationship, Parent Nationality,

    • If the parent is Ugandan, enter the National Identification Number.

    • If the parent is foreign, enter the Passport Number.

    • If the parent is a refugee, enter the Refugee ID Number.

  7. Click the Verify button to verify either Parent NIN or Passport Number or Refugee ID number.

  8. Enter the Parent/Guardian Phone Number

  9. Click the Transfer Learner button to complete the learner transfer process.
