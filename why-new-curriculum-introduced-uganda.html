Why the New Curriculum Was Introduced in Uganda

Uganda introduced the new curriculum to shift from rote learning to a competence-based approach that develops skills, creativity, and critical thinking in students. The aim is to prepare learners for real-life challenges and modern workplaces. With Shuleni Report Card System, schools can manage the new assessment structure efficiently and generate comprehensive reports.

Goals of the New Curriculum

  • Develop students’ competencies and generic skills
  • Encourage holistic development beyond academics
  • Promote practical learning and scenario-based assessment
  • Ensure continuous assessment and feedback throughout the term

How Shuleni Helps Schools

Shuleni automates calculations, tracks continuous assessment, and generates new curriculum report cards aligned with NCDC guidelines. Teachers can focus on teaching while parents get clear, accurate progress reports.

Conclusion

The new curriculum was introduced to modernize education in Uganda, focusing on skills, knowledge, and practical application. Shuleni Report Card Management System ensures that schools implement this curriculum efficiently, giving students the best chance to succeed.
